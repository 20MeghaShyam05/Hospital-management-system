# Patient Prescriptions Guide

## Purpose
This guide explains how patients can access their prescriptions and understand what the prescription contains in the Doctor-Patient Appointment Management System.

## What Is a Prescription
- A prescription is a record created by your doctor after a consultation.
- It contains the doctor's diagnosis, the medicines prescribed, post-consultation advice, and an optional follow-up date.
- Prescriptions are linked to a specific appointment.

## Viewing Your Prescriptions
- Patients may view all prescriptions issued to them using their patient ID.
- A patient may only view their own prescriptions. Viewing another patient's prescriptions is not allowed.
- Each prescription record shows the doctor's name, specialization, diagnosis, medicines, advice, follow-up date, and the date it was created.

## Prescription PDF and Google Drive
- After a consultation, the doctor or admin may save your prescription as a PDF to Google Drive.
- The system will automatically share the PDF with your registered email address.
- To find the prescription PDF, open Google Drive and look in the "Shared with me" section.
- If you cannot find the file, confirm that you are signed in with the same email address you registered with the hospital.

## Follow-Up Appointments
- If your prescription includes a follow-up date, you should book a new appointment on or near that date.
- You may book a follow-up appointment with the same doctor or another doctor as directed.
- Past-date and weekend appointments are not allowed through the normal booking flow.

## What a Prescription Does Not Include
- Prescriptions in this system do not include dosage instructions or a pharmacy-dispensing format.
- For full medicine dosage and administration instructions, ask your doctor directly during the consultation.
- If you have questions about your medicines after leaving the hospital, contact the hospital reception.

## AI Assistant and Prescriptions
- The AI assistant cannot view, create, or change your prescriptions.
- The AI assistant will not recommend specific medicines or dosages.
- For questions about your prescription, contact your treating doctor or the hospital admin.

## Support
- If you cannot see your prescription in the system, verify the appointment ID with the front desk.
- If the Google Drive PDF was not shared to your email, contact admin and confirm your registered email address is correct.
